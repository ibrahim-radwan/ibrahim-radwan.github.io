About Me

I am an active researcher in fields of Computer Vision and Image Understanding. I am passionate about designing and developing systems that automatically learn to recognize and interact in complex environments. My work aims to automatically understand and analyze human behavior as part of AI and as a critical factor in reaching a digital evolution in computational modeling. With years of experience spanning academia and industry, I've excelled as an educator at various universities. Brief Research Statement

As a dedicated AI researcher, I strive to advance human-object interaction in complex environments. With a decade of experience, including roles at the University of Canberra and the Australian National University, I've made a significant impact in "Human Activity Recognition," publishing 37 papers in top-tier avenues.
