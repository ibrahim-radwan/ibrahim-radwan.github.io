Brief Research Statement

As a dedicated AI researcher, I strive to advance human-object interaction in complex environments. With a decade of experience, including roles at the University of Canberra and the Australian National University, I've made a significant impact in "Human Activity Recognition," publishing 37 papers in top-tier avenues. Actively contributing to conferences and journals, I've secured over $A5.8M in research funding in the last two years, solidifying my position as a top-tier researcher with a focus on computational behavior analysis, aiming to contribute to the transformative potential of AI.
